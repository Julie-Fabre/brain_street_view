# Papers using Brain Street View

If you use Brain Street View in your research, please cite it using the [CITATION.cff](https://github.com/Julie-Fabre/brain_street_view/blob/main/CITATION.cff) file in the repository. If you'd like your paper listed here, please open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/Julie-Fabre/brain_street_view/issues) or pull request.

---

## Pan-Vazquez et al. (2025)

**VTA dopamine neuron activity produces spatially organized value representations**

Alejandro Pan-Vazquez, Christopher A. Zimmerman, Brenna McMannon, Julie M. J. Fabre, Miranta Louka, Tingying Jia, Yotam Sagiv, Steven J. West, Mayo Faulkner, International Brain Laboratory, Peter Dayan, Ilana B. Witten

*bioRxiv* (2025). DOI: [10.1101/2025.11.04.685995](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.11.04.685995)

BSV was used to characterize dopaminergic projections from the ventral tegmental area (VTA) to the striatum.

## Song & Peters (2025)

**Prefrontal cortex and striatum dissociate modality and learning order in cross-modal sensorimotor learning**

Da Song, Andrew J. Peters

*bioRxiv* (2025). DOI: [10.1101/2025.11.10.687671](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.11.10.687671)

BSV was used to examine prefrontal cortex projections to the striatum during cross-modal sensorimotor learning.

## Piantadosi et al. (2025)

**Risk reshapes amygdala representation of choice**

Patrick T. Piantadosi, Kendall M. Coden, Hyesun Choi, Sarah J. Perry, Marcelle Halfeld, Julia A. Schaffer, Jeff P. Goff, Nathen A. Spitz, Nicole R. Schwab, Rodrigo Sandon, Shanzeh Sadiq, Maddison M. Devine, Vincent D. Costa, Daniel da Silva, Andrew Holmes

*bioRxiv* (2025). DOI: [10.1101/2025.10.06.680813](https://doi.org/10.1101/2025.10.06.680813)

BSV was used to visualize amygdala connectivity patterns in the context of risk-based decision making.
